Our Story

Woven by hand, built to last

Babu Cane Works has stood in Kumar Nagar Colony for years, hand-weaving cane and rattan into sofas, chairs, swings, beds and storage pieces for homes across Chittoor. Every frame is shaped and woven by hand, not machine-pressed.

We work with natural cane sourced and seasoned the traditional way, and we still build to order — your size, your finish, your cushion colour.
